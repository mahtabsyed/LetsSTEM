Who can attend these classes ?
Kids between 8 - 15 yrs
Are any pre-requisites e.g. should the kids know some coding ?
Any kid can join the classes and will be able to create small projects from 1st class... Based on their interest and capability they will grow to the next level at their pace - Foundation -> Beginners -> Intermediate -> Advanced
Do the kids need to come with a laptop ?
If they have a laptop its fine, else we can provide one... With parents agreed permission we will create their accounts in Scratch, Github, Email id etc. So after the class parents can proudly see what thier kids have created.
